Saving's Never Been So Easy

Have you ever wondered why you never have anything left from your paychecks? Or does it always seem like it’s just a waiting game until the next check, or it’s a picking game between which bills get paid this month because you just can’t afford to pay. Well if you’re anything like me, then we’re in the same boat and you may be asking yourself how to switch things up to take control of your finances.

Learning where my money goes and tracking it was the first step to my journey. Understanding this is a huge part because once you’re able to sit down and figure out what it is you’re spending your money on, then you’ll be able to figure out where you’re able to make cut backs and create opportunity for savings.

So what’s this website for?
As a developer, I realized that there are not many online tools to help with savings challenges to track your progress. Theres plenty of options to get hands on books and PDF versions but I wanted something I could fill out online, thus the idea to create this site came along!

So how does it work?
Considering taking on your finances is a big step, getting all the help you can is everything. On the resources page, you’ll be able to find different links and videos for help with your financial journey as well as additional resources to learn more about savings challenges! To actually access the challenges you’ll want create an account to get started and then pick a challenge to go from there!
